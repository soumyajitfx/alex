About Me

Designing with
purpose

Hello — I'm Alex Mercer, a 26-year-old UI/UX designer and front-end developer based in San Francisco. I bring ideas to life through thoughtful design systems, clean code, and a relentless attention to detail.

My approach merges aesthetic rigor with technical precision. Every spacing decision, every animation timing, every color choice is intentional. I believe great interfaces feel inevitable — as if they couldn't exist any other way.

Beyond design, I'm deeply interested in how AI tools are reshaping creative work — from generative design to intelligent prototyping. I actively incorporate these into my workflow to push the boundaries of what's possible.
